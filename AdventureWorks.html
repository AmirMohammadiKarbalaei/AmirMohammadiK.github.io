AdventureWorks

CONCEPT

The principal objective of this project is to address six key questions posed by stakeholders (Generation UK and Ireland) within the framework of Adventure Works database. Employing tools such as Python and SQL, the project will leverage their capabilities for data exploration, analysis and visualization. The initial phase involves a comprehensive understanding of the questions provided by the instructor, followed by the systematic gathering of requisite data from the Adventure Works dataset. Subsequent efforts will focus on a meticulous exploration and cleaning process to ensure the dataset's integrity and readiness for analysis.

DATA

Presenting the Adventure Works Database, a widely-used sample dataset in the realm of Microsoft products. This fictitious data repository serves as an invaluable resource for training, testing, and demonstrating functionalities within SQL Server and other Microsoft platforms. Designed to simulate real-world scenarios, the Adventure Works database includes tables representing key business entities such as customers, products, orders, and employees. By leveraging this dataset, users and developers gain practical insights into database operations and the construction of queries and reports. Importantly, Adventure Works allows for hands-on learning without the use of actual proprietary or sensitive data.

PROCESS

In an ever-changing market landscape, customer contentment is closely linked to the smooth integration of product excellence, efficient delivery, and the overall service encounter. This Project aims to transform the comprehension and improvement of customer contentment by exploring the domains of delivery and review sentiment.

  • Delivery Period Evaluation
  • Review Sentiment Analysis
