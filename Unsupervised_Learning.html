Customer segmentation

CONCEPT

In this project, the focus is on leveraging advanced clustering algorithms to partition a company's customer base into distinct groups that share similarities. Customer segmentation is a strategic approach aimed at discerning patterns and characteristics within the customer population. The ultimate goal is to tailor business strategies to each segment, maximising the overall value of customers to the organisation.

DATA

The dataset used for customer segmentation comprises detailed information about individual transactions, providing valuable insights into customer behavior. Here's a breakdown of the key columns:

  • invoice_no: A unique identifier for each transaction.
  • customer_id: Unique identification for each customer.
  • gender: The gender of the customer.
  • age: Age of the customer.
  • category: The category of the purchased item (e.g., Clothing, Shoes, Books).
  • quantity: The quantity of items purchased in a specific transaction.
  • price: The total price of the transaction.
  • payment_method: The method used for payment (e.g., Credit Card, Debit Card, Cash).
  • invoice_date: The date of the transaction.
  • shopping_mall: The shopping mall where the transaction took place.
